🏅 6 Golden Rules of Publishing
01.Lead with curiosity, not the title. Mystery hooks outperform branded copy. Save the explicit reveal for the caption or 2nd slide.
02.Trailers are TikTok gold. Teaser/Trailer formats deliver 2–5× the ER of clips. Every new title needs a TikTok-native cut before launch week.
03.Carousels beat single images on Instagram. Carousels averaged 4.5–5.9% ER vs. 3.9–4.3% for photos when matched for content quality.
04.Monday is peak day. 3 of the top 10 Instagram posts published Monday Jun 15. Front-load best creative early in the week to maximize algorithmic momentum.
05.Fewer posts, higher engagement. W1 (111 posts) outperformed W2 (153 posts) by 49% in engagements. Enforce a 120-post weekly hard cap across all platforms.
06.Cross-cultural reactive content builds loyalty. The Lamine Yamal World Cup TikTok and Criminopatía X post show that tapping real-time cultural moments drives authentic engagement.
